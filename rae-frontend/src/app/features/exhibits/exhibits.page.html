PlastiCulture is a pop-up museum-type exhibit that invites visitors to explore the history of plastic. They will have the opportunity to look at how plastic use is harming the environment and our health, and in which industries it improves our quality of life. Participants will be challenged to increase their awareness of the use of plastics in all aspects of their lives, and to find ways to reduce their use of plastics. They will walk out of the exhibit with greater knowledge of the impact of different types of plastics on the environment, and with tools to make changes in their habits, as well as to encourage change in plastic use in local businesses and their communities.
