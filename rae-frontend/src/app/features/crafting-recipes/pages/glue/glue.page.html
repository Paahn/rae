Plastic-Free Crafting Glue

What you'll need:

  • 2 cups water
  • 3/4 cups white flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • a pot
  • a whisk

How to make it:

  1. Combine flour with room temperature water. Place over heat and stir constantly.
  2. Once it is warm, add the sugar and stir constantly until the sugar is melted.
  3. Add the vinegar and continue stirring. Then add the baking soda and continue stirring. The mixture will begin to bubble due to the reaction of the vinegar and baking soda.
  4. Keep stirring over the heat until your glue is smooth and has thickened.Then, remove from the heat and allow to cool.
  5. Transfer to a glass jar and store in the fridge for up to 3 months.

Note: This glue works well for crafting with paper and cardboard.
