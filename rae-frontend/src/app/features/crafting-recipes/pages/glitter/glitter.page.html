Plastic-Free Glitter

What you'll need:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 8-10 drops of food colouring
  • tin foil

How to make it:

  1. Combine sugar and food colouring in a bowl and mix well. Fold the edges of the tin foil up to make a bowl. Place on a baking sheet and pour the sugar mixture in the foil bowl.
  2. Bake at 250 Farenheit for 8 minutes. Store in a glass jar.

Note: Because it is made from sugar, allow glue to dry a bit and become tacky before applying glitter.
