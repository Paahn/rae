Make Your Own Bioplastic

What youâ€™ll need:

1 cup of whole milk 1 tbsp white vinegar a glass bowl a sieve cheese cloth paper towel

How to make it:

  1. Line your sieve with cheese cloth and set over a bowl.
  2. Warm the milk in a microwave for 1 minute, or until bubbly.
  3. Remove from the microwave and add the vinegar. Stir until you see the solids separate from the whey, or liquid part of the milk.
  4. Pour the separated mixture into your cheese cloth lined sieve and set aside for 20 minutes to drain.
  5. Place the casein on the paper towels and squeeze out as much liquid as possible.
  6. Flatten and cut shapes, or mould into any shape you wish.
  7. Place on parchment paper to dry for 24 to 48 hours.
